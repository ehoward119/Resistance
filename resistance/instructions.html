Secret Sagehen Resistance!

Don't know how to play?

Secret Sagehen Resistance is a spinoff of the popular board game "Resistance"

In short, there are two teams trying to win: The Stagthenas, and the Sagehens

Overall gameplay

Players will have to go on "missions" decide who is coming along with them

The mission leader will select who goes on what mission. Depending on the stage of the game, there is a different amount of players needed

Once a mission team is decided upon, players going on the mission will secretely vote to fail or pass the mission

Most missions need 1 failure to fail

Once there are 3 failures or 3 sucesses, the game is over

Stagthenas

You are the good guys

Your objective is to have at least 3 missions succeed

Thus, every time you're selected on a mission you should vote to pass

As a member of the Stagthenas you want to figure out who the Sagehens are to prevent mission failure

Sagehens

You are the bad guys

Your objective is to have 3 missions fail

You can choose to fail or pass a missions

Conceal your identity to the best of your skills
